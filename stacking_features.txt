CFB Pro Stacking Model
========================================

Architecture: StackingRegressor (Experts + Boss)

THE EXPERTS (Base Models):
  1. Gradient (HistGradientBoostingRegressor)
     - Handles NaN natively
     - max_iter: 100, max_depth: 6

  2. Forest (RandomForestRegressor)
     - Pipeline: Imputer -> RF
     - n_estimators: 100, max_depth: 10

  3. Linear (Ridge)
     - Pipeline: Imputer -> Scaler -> Ridge
     - alpha: 1.0

THE BOSS (Meta Model):
  - RidgeCV with cross-validation
  - Learns optimal expert weights

Training: 2022-2024 (11234 games)
Testing: 2025 (3742 games)
Single Model MAE: 13.79 points
Stacking MAE: 13.80 points
Improvement: -0.01 points
